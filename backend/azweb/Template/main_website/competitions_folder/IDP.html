About

The aim of this competition is to expose students to the real life industrial problems. The competition expects participants to prepare an abstract for their approach of their solution and present in on the day of symposium to the judges.

Problem Statement

Structure

  • Stage 1: Abstract submission
  • Stage 2: Presentation
  • Maximum of 3 participants allowed per team. Students of different colleges can form a team.
  • Both UGs and PGs can form a team
  • All the members should be majoring in Chemical Engineering
  • The students will be shortlisted for presentation round based on their abstracts

Incentives

  • The winning team will get an Internship offer based on the quality of overall work.
  • Top 3 teams will be awarded exciting Cash Prizes worth 25K and Certificates.
  • Top team with all Undergraduates will get a special cash prize.
  • Top 5 teams will receive Certificate of Appreciation.
  • Certificate of Participation will be given to all teams submitting an abstract.

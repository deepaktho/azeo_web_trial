Important Instructions

  • Before you start, please check the problem statement to understand the structure, rules & regulations of the Chemathon competition.
  • You need to fill out your Team ID to submit.
  • Enter decimal values. Don’t enter fractions.
  • If the answer you entered is within the acceptable range, "The answer falls within an acceptable range.” will be shown. Otherwise, “Answer is out of range” will be shown.
  • If any of your answers are not in the acceptable range, you will not be able to submit. To submit, leave the blanks empty for those questions.
  • You can revisit the page to attempt the remaining questions even if you submit.
  • Your answers will be saved in our database when you submit, but your answers will not be visible when you revisit the page.
  • If you lose internet connection/refresh or close the page, your answers will not be saved in our database and will not be visible when you revisit the portal.
  • Make sure to note down your answers somewhere.
  • You can submit even if you haven’t solved all the problems. Your final submission should include answers to all the questions you have solved and wish to submit. Your latest submission will be considered for evaluation.
  • There is no limitation on the number of submissions.
  • Weightage for parameter values is only 20% per question. So, you have to invest time in the README file and code. Remember README & Code files have a weightage of 80% per question.
  • If you have any queries, please fill out this Query Form. We will check your query, resolve it as soon as possible and get back to you. If the query is general, we will add that to the General Query Sheet along with our response.
  • After you finish solving the problems, upload the ZIP (Code & README files) file here link.
  • Submission deadline: 11:00 PM March 20, 2022
